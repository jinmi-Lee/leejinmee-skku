Project Overview

Korean Variety Show Trend Dashboard

A Streamlit-based analytics dashboard that examines the popularity, audience engagement, and online trends of Korean variety shows using real-world data sources such as YouTube, Google Trends, social media, and TV rating websites.

📺 YouTube Data

Analyze views, likes, comments, subscriber growth, and video performance.

Monitor audience reactions, sentiment, and online discussions.

Problem Statement

Korean variety shows generate significant online engagement and influence through YouTube and social media platforms. However, popularity, engagement metrics, and trending topics are scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult to compare programs and cast members effectively.

Proposed Solution

Phase 1

Aggregate data from multiple online platforms into a unified dashboard.

Phase 2

Create interactive visualizations and ranking systems using Plotly.

Phase 3

Generate meaningful insights about audience engagement and trend evolution.
