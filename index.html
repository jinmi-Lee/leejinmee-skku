Korean Variety Show Trend Dashboard

Film and Media · SKKU · AI & Data Visualization Project

About Me

I am a Film and Media student at SKKU. I am interested in how Korean variety shows communicate with audiences through humor, editing styles, subtitles, and cast chemistry. Through this project, I want to explore how Korean variety content becomes popular and spreads through YouTube, social media, and online communities.

Project Overview

This project is a Streamlit web dashboard that analyzes the popularity and online trends of Korean variety shows. The dashboard uses sample data and visualizations to compare programs, cast members, audience engagement, and trending keywords.

Problem Statement

Korean variety shows create many online trends and attract large audiences through YouTube and social media. However, it is difficult to easily compare popularity, audience engagement, and trending topics across different programs and cast members.

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is a 3-phase Streamlit dashboard that presents interactive visualizations and meaningful insights about Korean variety entertainment trends.

Key Features

📈 Popularity Trend Analysis
🏆 Cast Member Ranking
🔥 Keyword & Meme Analysis
❤️ Viewer Engagement Analysis
⚖️ Interactive Show Comparison
